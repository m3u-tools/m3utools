EPG .gz Only Merger

Upload up to 5 files (Only .gz) to merge:

How to use.

Enter files 5 at a time max.

Hit merge and download.

Hit Download merge EPG

When downloaded you can simple upload to dropbox or similar then shorten url using bit.ly or short url or any similar services.

THIS DOES NOT AUTOMATICALLY UPDATE EPG WHEN THEY ARE EXPIRED YOU NEED TO REMAKE OR FIND A SCRIPT TO AUTOMATICALLY DO IT. Each time you make name it the same reupload to Dropbox or similar service and youll be done it will use the same url.

Put this at the top of your playlist if you want it to load with your playlist. "Note, tvg-id in your playlist needs to match your epg to auto populate. Or else you will need to set it manually on your player such as tivimate.

#EXTM3U url-tvg="link to epg here
